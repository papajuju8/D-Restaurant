D' Owner

Hi! I'm Julius, the creator of this website, and the owner of D' Restaurant. I was inspired to create this theme because of my newly discovered hobby during this quarantine season - COOKING. Due to my health problem, eating nutritious foods became a part of my lifestyle. After discovering 101 Cookbooks, I was amazed with how healthy foods were made into something "special".
